About Me

My name is Nicolette Renner, but I go by Niki. I'm 27 years old and live in West Virginia. I've been a union construction worker, Ironworker specifically, for the last six years. I love my job and I've worked alongside some great people. But what inspired me to learn programming was my need for a creative outlet, and my desire to secure a safer, more stable career. I decided to take the plunge and enroll in an online bootcamp offered by UNC Charlotte, which I am set to graduate in October of 2022. I've always been a hard worker, and I can't wait to see where this adventure will take me!
